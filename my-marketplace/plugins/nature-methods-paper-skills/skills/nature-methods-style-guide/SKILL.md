# Nature-Methods Style Guide

Common style reference for all nature-methods paper writing agents.
Based on analysis of 14 papers in Hydrogen ICE / Dual-Fuel Engine research.

## Quick Reference

- **Citation**: Numbered brackets [1], [2,3], [4-6]
- **Voice**: Passive dominant in Methods/Results; active in Introduction context
- **Tense**: Past for experiments/findings; present for established facts and figure references
- **Person**: Third person; use "this study" instead of "we"
- **Avg sentence length**: 16-18 words
