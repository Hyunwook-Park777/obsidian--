# Discussion Template

## Note on Section Structure

In the analyzed corpus, Discussion is typically combined with Results as "Results and Discussion" (see results-template.md). When a separate Discussion section exists, it follows this pattern:

## Structure: Summary of Findings → Comparison with Literature → Implications → Limitations → Future Work

### Summary of Findings
- Restate key results concisely
- "The results showed that [finding]."
- "The [approach] effectively [achieved goal]."

### Comparison with Literature
- Compare with previous studies
- "These results are consistent with [Author et al. [ref]]."
- "Unlike [previous study], the present study showed [difference]."
- "The [X]% improvement exceeds the [Y]% reported by [Author et al. [ref]]."

### Implications
- Explain practical significance
- "[Strategy] could be a solution considering [practical aspect]."
- "The main findings from this study can provide a practical solution for [application]."

### Limitations
- Acknowledge study limitations
- "It is noted that the [condition] could be changed in consideration of [factors]."
- "The [limitation] should be addressed in future studies."

### Future Work
- State planned or recommended next steps
- "The future work is to [verb] the [topic] by [method]."
- "[Approach] will be also implemented to [goal]."
