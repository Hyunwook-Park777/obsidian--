## Field-Specific Technical Terms

- brake thermal efficiency (BTE)
- indicated mean effective pressure (IMEP)
- brake mean effective pressure (BMEP)
- heat release rate (HRR)
- maximum pressure rise rate (MPRR)
- mass fraction burned (MFB)
- coefficient of variation (COV)
- degree of constant volume (DOCV)

### Engine Timing
- crank angle degree (CAD)
- top dead center (TDC)
- after top dead center (aTDC)
- start of injection (SOI)
- spark timing / ignition timing

### Mixture/Combustion
- excess air ratio (lambda)
- equivalence ratio (phi)
- exhaust gas recirculation (EGR)
- volumetric efficiency
- compression ratio

### Hydrogen-Specific
- backfire
- pre-ignition
- knock / knocking
- abnormal combustion
- port fuel injection (PFI)
- direct injection (DI)
- low-pressure direct injection (LPDI)

### Dual-Fuel Specific
- conventional dual fuel (CDF)
- reactivity-controlled compression ignition (RCCI)
- premixed charge compression ignition (PCCI)
- natural gas substitution ratio (SR)
